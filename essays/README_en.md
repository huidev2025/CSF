# CSF Column Shelf / The CSF Column

[简体中文](README.md) &#124; **English**

This is the deep intellectual archive of CSF (Collaboration Specification Framework) — documenting the engineering principles behind treating AI as a genuine *collaborator*, and the philosophical evolution that emerged as the methodology bootstrapped itself through practice.

---

## Flagship Series: Working with Intelligence

> "When the parameters and context window of a large model are already large enough to hold your entire codebase, why does human-AI collaboration still drift? Because you are torturing a probabilistically-native new colleague with the physical rules of the old world."

This series is the **v4.0 flagship manifesto** of the CSF system. It is no longer abstract academic derivation — it cuts in from the serious software engineering angle of four major systems (Semantic Management, Collaborative Communication, Quality Assurance, Evolution System), using highly concrete engineering metaphors and a rigorous theoretical architecture to show you how to work with this "bad-memory, probabilistically-native, fragile yet highly active" intelligent life form.

## Reading Notes

This column is the **intellectual soul** of the CSF method — it does not represent the standard operations you must know to start using it (that is what [QUICKSTART.md](../QUICKSTART.md) does).

If you find yourself overwhelmed by the high density of concepts, don't worry at all:
* First go replicate [csf-minimal/](../csf-minimal) for a 30-second A/B experience comparison.
* Use this framework to build a real small product with AI (even a mini-program with just a few files).
* When you are repeatedly tortured by AI losing context and start cursing it as "stupid" — come back and read the corresponding chapter in this column. You'll find that every clause is a bloody antidote forged in a late-night blood-pressure spike.
